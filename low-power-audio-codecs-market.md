<p><p>The Low Power Audio Codecs market is segmented into analog and digital categories. Analog codecs process audio signals in their continuous form, focusing on simplicity and power efficiency, making them ideal for basic audio applications. In contrast, digital codecs convert audio signals into binary data, enabling advanced features like compression and enhanced audio quality. Digital codecs are prevalent in modern devices, supporting complex audio processing with lower power consumption, making them suitable for portable electronics and IoT devices.</p></p>
