<p><p>Electric scooter sharing systems are typically categorized into dockless and station-based markets. In contrast, station-based systems require users to rent and return scooters at specific docking stations, offering more control over scooter distribution and parking. Both models cater to urban mobility needs, aiming to reduce traffic congestion, promote sustainable transport, and enhance last-mile connectivity.</p></p>
