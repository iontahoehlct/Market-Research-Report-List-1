<p><ul><li>Un-Cooled Thermal Imaging</li><li>Cryogenically Cooled Thermal Imaging</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The thermal imaging market is primarily divided into two types: uncooled thermal imaging and cryogenically cooled thermal imaging. Uncooled thermal imaging utilizes sensors that operate at ambient temperatures, making them more compact and cost-effective, ideal for applications like surveillance and automotive uses. In contrast, cryogenically cooled thermal imaging involves cooling sensors to extremely low temperatures, enhancing sensitivity and resolution, making them suitable for demanding applications like military and scientific research. Each type serves distinct needs based on performance requirements and environmental conditions.</p></p>
