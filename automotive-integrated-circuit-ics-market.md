<p><strong>The Automotive Integrated Circuit (ICs) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Monolithic Integrated circuits</li><li>Hybrid Integrated circuits</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive integrated circuit (IC) market comprises primarily two types: monolithic and hybrid integrated circuits. Monolithic ICs are fabricated on a single semiconductor substrate, offering compactness, lower costs, and enhanced reliability, making them ideal for applications like engine control and infotainment systems. In contrast, hybrid ICs combine multiple components, often including discrete devices, onto a single substrate, providing flexibility and customization for specialized applications such as advanced driver-assistance systems (ADAS) and power management. Both types enable the growing complexity in automotive electronics.</p></p>
