<p><strong>The Crystalline Dextrose Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Monohydrate glucose</li><li>Anhydrous glucose</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The crystalline dextrose market consists primarily of two types: monohydrate glucose and anhydrous glucose. Monohydrate glucose contains one molecule of water per glucose unit, making it suitable for various food and pharmaceutical applications due to its stability and solubility. In contrast, anhydrous glucose lacks water content, resulting in a more concentrated and energy-dense product. Both types are widely used in the food industry as sweeteners, energy sources, and texturizers, as well as in medical applications for intravenous solutions.</p></p>
