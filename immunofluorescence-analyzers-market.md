<p><strong>The Immunofluorescence Analyzers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Enzyme Linked Fluorescent Immunoassay</li><li>Chemiluminescence Immunoassay</li><li>Radioimmunoassay</li><li>Enzyme Linked Immunosorbent Assay</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Immunofluorescence analyzers are crucial in diagnostic and research applications, utilizing various assay types. Enzyme-linked fluorescent immunoassays (ELFIA) leverage enzyme-substrate reactions to produce fluorescent signals, allowing for sensitive detection. Chemiluminescence immunoassays (CLIA) use light-emitting reactions for quantifying analytes, offering high sensitivity. Radioimmunoassays (RIA) employ radioactive isotopes for sensitive measurement of antigens or hormones. Enzyme-linked immunosorbent assays (ELISA) use enzymes to produce measurable signals, commonly employed in diverse clinical and research settings for antigen detection.</p></p>
