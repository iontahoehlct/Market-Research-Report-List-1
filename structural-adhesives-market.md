<p><p>The structural adhesives market encompasses various adhesive types, each suited for specific applications. Epoxy adhesives offer strong bonding and chemical resistance, ideal for metal and composite materials. Polyurethane adhesives provide flexibility and durability, often used in construction and automotive sectors. Acrylic adhesives are known for their fast curing times and versatility, suitable for plastics and metals. Methyl methacrylate adhesives offer exceptional bonding strength and temperature resistance, making them popular in industrial applications. Cyanoacrylate adhesives are fast-setting and effective for small repairs and bonding dissimilar materials.</p></p>
