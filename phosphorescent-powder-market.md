<p><ul><li>Short Persistence Phosphorescent Pigment</li><li>Long Persistence Phosphorescent Pigment</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The phosphorescent powder market is categorized into short persistence and long persistence phosphorescent pigments. Short persistence pigments glow for a brief period, typically lasting from a few seconds to a few minutes, making them suitable for emergency signage and low-light applications. In contrast, long persistence pigments emit light for extended durations, ranging from several hours to days, ideal for applications like glow-in-the-dark products, outdoor signage, and safety equipment. Each type caters to specific needs based on luminosity duration and application.</p></p>
