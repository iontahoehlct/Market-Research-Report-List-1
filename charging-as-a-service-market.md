<p><p>Charging as a Service encompasses three main market types: Level 1, Level 2, and Level 3 charging. Level 1 charging uses standard household outlets, suitable for home use and slow charging, typically for overnight use. Level 2 charging provides faster charging solutions, often found in public spaces and commercial locations, catering to electric vehicle users during short stops. Level 3 charging, or DC fast charging, offers rapid charging capabilities, making it ideal for long-distance travel and high-utilization scenarios.</p></p>
