<p><p>The articulated arm machine market is primarily divided into two types: pneumatic articulated arm machines and electric articulated arm machines. Pneumatic articulated arm machines utilize compressed air to power their movements, making them suitable for applications that require lightweight operation and rapid cycles. In contrast, electric articulated arm machines rely on electric motors, offering precise control, higher torque, and enhanced programmability for complex tasks. Both types are vital in various industries, including manufacturing, robotics, and automated assembly processes.</p></p>
