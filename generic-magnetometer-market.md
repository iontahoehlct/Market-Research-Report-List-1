<p><ul><li>Scalar Magnetometer</li><li>Vector Magnetometer</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The generic magnetometer market is categorized into three main types: scalar magnetometers, vector magnetometers, and others. Scalar magnetometers measure the magnitude of magnetic fields without directional information, making them suitable for applications like mineral exploration. Vector magnetometers, on the other hand, capture both magnitude and direction, allowing for more detailed geophysical surveys and navigation. The "others" category includes specialized magnetometers that employ unique technology for niche applications, such as airborne surveys or industrial monitoring. Each type plays a crucial role in various scientific and industrial fields.</p></p>
