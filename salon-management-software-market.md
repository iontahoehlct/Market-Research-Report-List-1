<p><ul><li>Cloud-based</li><li>On-premises</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Salon management software is categorized into two main types: cloud-based and on-premises solutions. Cloud-based software is hosted online, allowing for remote access and real-time updates, making it ideal for salons that prioritize flexibility and mobility. It typically requires a subscription model. On-premises software, on the other hand, is installed locally on salon computers, offering greater control and security over data. However, it may involve higher upfront costs and limited accessibility outside the salon environment. Each type serves different operational needs.</p></p>
