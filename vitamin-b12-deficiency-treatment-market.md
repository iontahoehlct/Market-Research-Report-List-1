<p><p>Vitamin B12 deficiency treatment predominantly involves two forms: Hydroxocobalamin and Cyanocobalamin. In contrast, Cyanocobalamin is a synthetic variant commonly available in oral supplements and injections, valued for its stability and cost-effectiveness. Both forms effectively replenish B12 levels, catering to different patient needs, contributing to the diverse vitamin B12 treatment market.</p></p>
