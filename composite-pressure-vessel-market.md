<p><p>The composite pressure vessel market is segmented into Type III and Type IV vessels, along with other types. Type III vessels use a metal liner wrapped with a composite material, offering a balance of weight and pressure resistance. Type IV vessels utilize a fully composite liner, providing higher lightweight properties and corrosion resistance, making them ideal for high-pressure storage of gases. Other types include hybrid or specialty vessels designed for specific applications, catering to diverse industry needs and technologies.</p></p>
