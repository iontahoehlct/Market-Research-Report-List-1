<p><p>The spinal imaging market is primarily categorized into four modalities: MRI, CT, X-ray, and ultrasound. MRI provides detailed soft tissue images, making it essential for diagnosing spinal abnormalities. CT scans offer rapid and precise cross-sectional images, ideal for complex fractures. X-ray is a cost-effective, quick method for evaluating spinal alignment and detecting basic issues. Ultrasound, while less common, is useful for guiding spinal injections and assessing certain conditions. Together, these technologies support comprehensive spinal health diagnostics.</p></p>
