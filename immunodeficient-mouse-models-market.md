Traditional immunodeficient models, such as SCID and Nude mice, have limited immune responses and are widely used for tumor studies. Severe immunodeficient models, like NOD/SCID and NSG mice, exhibit even more profound immunodeficiency, allowing for more complex human cell engraftments. Next-generation severe immunodeficient models further enhance these characteristics, enabling sophisticated research into human diseases and therapeutic interventions.</p></p>
