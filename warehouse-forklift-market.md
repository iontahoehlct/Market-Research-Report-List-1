<p><p>The warehouse forklift market comprises two main types: electric forklifts and internal combustion forklifts. Electric forklifts are powered by batteries, offering quieter operation and zero emissions, making them suitable for indoor applications. They excel in environments requiring cleanliness and reduced noise levels. Internal combustion forklifts, powered by gasoline or diesel, are typically used for outdoor applications due to their robustness and ability to handle heavier loads. They excel in rugged conditions but emit harmful pollutants, limiting their indoor usage.</p></p>
