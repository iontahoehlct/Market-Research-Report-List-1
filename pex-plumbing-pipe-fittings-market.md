<p><p>PEX plumbing pipes and fittings are categorized into three main types: PEX-A, PEX-B, and PEX-C, differentiated by their manufacturing processes and properties. PEX-A is highly flexible, allowing for tighter bends and thermal expansion resistance. PEX-B, produced through a different method, is more rigid and offers enhanced resistance to high pressure, while PEX-C is known for its chemical resistance and affordability. Each type serves distinct applications in residential and commercial plumbing, catering to varying installation needs and performance criteria.</p></p>
