<p><p>Web POS software is categorized into two main market types: cloud-based and on-premise. Cloud-based systems operate on remote servers, allowing access from any device with internet connectivity, offering benefits such as automatic updates and scalability. In contrast, on-premise software is installed directly on local servers, providing greater control and security but often requiring significant upfront investment. Each type caters to different business needs, with cloud solutions favoring flexibility, while on-premise options appeal to those prioritizing data control.</p></p>
