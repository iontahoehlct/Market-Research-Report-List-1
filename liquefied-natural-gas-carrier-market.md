<p><p>A Liquefied Natural Gas Carrier (LNG Carrier) is a specialized maritime vessel designed for the transportation of liquefied natural gas. These carriers are equipped with advanced cryogenic technology to maintain the natural gas in a liquid state at extremely low temperatures, typically around -162°C. The growth of the LNG carrier market is driven by the increasing global demand for cleaner energy sources, as countries seek to reduce carbon emissions and transition to more sustainable energy options.</p><p>Market growth is further supported by expanding LNG infrastructure, including terminals and regasification facilities, and the rise of emerging economies adopting natural gas as a primary energy source. As a result of these factors, the Liquefied Natural Gas Carrier Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 10.2% during the forecast period. </p><p>Recent trends indicate a focus on building larger, more efficient carriers to optimize transportation costs and minimize environmental impact. Innovations in hull design and propulsion systems are also gaining traction, improving the overall efficiency and reliability of LNG carriers.
