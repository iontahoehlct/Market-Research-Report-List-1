<p><ul><li>N4 Nickel Mesh</li><li>N6 Nickel Mesh</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Nickel wire mesh for water electrolyzers is categorized primarily into N4 and N6 mesh types. N4 nickel mesh features a medium pore size, providing a balanced surface area that enhances the efficiency of gas evolution during electrolysis. In contrast, N6 nickel mesh has a finer weave, improving electrode surface characteristics for greater catalytic activity and reduced resistance. Both types are integral in improving the performance, durability, and overall efficiency of water electrolyzers in various industrial applications.</p></p>
